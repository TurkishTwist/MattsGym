About Us

Matt's Gym is a locally owned and operated gym created by me, Matthew Zemelka. Being apart of many people's fitness journey and helping reach goals that some felt were never attainable; I hold this part of my life the closest to my heart. I wanted to create a reasonable priced gym where anybody can come and achieve there goals, no matter how high the personal bar is set. My team here is personally selected by who I think can inspire the most change in others, and who also hold this passion like no other. Come share your passion with the family.
