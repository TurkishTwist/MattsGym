Pricing

 

Basic Package - $29.99

The Basic Package allows for the use of our state of the art equipment during working hours of the day.

 

Mid-Range Package - $45

The Mid-Range Package allows use of not only the equipment, but the world-class amenities provided at the gym as well. Not only will you be able to have access to products here at Matt's Gym, but you're welcome to come in past working hours any day of the week.

Premium Membership - $169

You're not a full member of Matt's Gym unless you're a premium member. By committing your fitness and well-being to Matt's gym, you're provided the biggest opportunity to accessing the best version of you. Premium Members are given access to any coaching needed and can appear at any class given at the gym at any time. Life can get busy sometimes, but no matter the time of day Matt's Gym is committed to providing coaching.
