Amenities

Matt's Gym offers nothing but the best in terms of amenities for our valued members. When it comes to rest and recovery we have you covered.

Hot Tub

Our elegant hot tub setups pulsate at increasing temperatures ideal for muscles recovery and relaxation. With varying temperatures, our hot tub can assist with strictly muscle fatigue or even relaxation.

Sauna

From our cardio-junkies to our power lifters, the sauna is a great place to end the day. Our premium sauna salts will help sweat out toxins and replenish your skin unlike any other. With optimal temperature always put in place and sterilized benches, our sauna will turn your great workout into a great day.

Pool

Unwind and take a swim. Our 50ft crystal clear pool is perfect for getting your cardio in or cooling down your muscles naturally. Matt's Gym is made for our members, and nothing cements that idea like providing our members with an amazing water experience year-round.
